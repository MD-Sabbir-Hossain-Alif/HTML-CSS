IPhone 16

The iPhone 16 features a 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR OLED display, powered by the advanced A18 chip for smooth and efficient performance. It includes a 48MP main camera, 12MP ultrawide, and a 12MP front camera. With iOS 18, 5G support, and IP68 water resistance, it offers strong battery life, sleek design, and excellent everyday functionality.

  • Chipset Apple A18 Bionic (3 nm)
  • CPU Hexa-core (2x4.04 GHz + 4x2.20 GHz)
  • Apple GPU (5-core graphics)
  • Dual 12M, 48 MP, f/1.6, 26mm (wide), 1/1.56"
  • Display 1708 nits max brightness (measured)
  • Battery Active use score 15:42h
  • USB Type-C 2.0, DisplayPort
